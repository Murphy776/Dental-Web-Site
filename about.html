Dr. Daniel J. Wols D.D.S

Dr. Wols grew up in Plainfield Illinois before attending Loyola University of Chicago, where he graduated with a Bachelor's of Science Degree in Biology, in 1989. He went on to attend the University of Illinois, College of Dentistry. There he received both an additional Bachelor's degree in Dentistry, as well as his Doctorate of Dental Surgery (D.D.S.) in 1993. Calling the Orland area his home, has been an easy fit for Dr. Wols, his wife, and four daughters over the last 16 years. After over 20 years in practice, Dr. Wols continues to advance his knowledge of dentistry with continuing education classes focusing primarily on implant dentistry, cosmetic dentistry, and new dental techniques and materials. He is proud to call himself a family dentist, providing a full spectrum of dental care, while building long lasting relationships with families and patients of all ages. Implant and cosmetic cases are particularly rewarding for him. He'd welcome the opportunity to become your new family dentist.
