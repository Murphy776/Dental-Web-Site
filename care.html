The following is a summary of the array of dental services we routinely perform at Orland Center.

Preventative dental care:

  • Dental exam
  • Radiographs (X-rays)
  • Prophylaxis (dental cleaning)
  • Fluoride Treatment
  • Sealants

Periodontal Treatment:

  • Periodontal screening (evaluating for gum disease)
  • Scaling and Root Planing
  • Periodontal maintenance

Cosmetic Procedures:

  • Composite restorations (tooth-colored fillings)
  • Porcelain veneers
  • Porcelain crowns (caps)
  • Porcelain bridges
  • Dental bleaching

Endodontic therapy:

  • Root Canals

Oral Surgery:

  • Simple extractions

Restorative dentistry:

  • Composite fillings
  • Crowns (caps)
  • Bridges
  • Implant supported crowns and bridges
  • Implant supported dentures
  • Restoration using "all-on-four" technique
  • Traditional full and partial dentures

Bruxism and TMJ appliances
