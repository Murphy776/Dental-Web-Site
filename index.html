Welcome


At Orland Dental Center our mission is to provide the highest quality dentistry in a comfortable environment where you can feel at ease. Both of our doctors have over twenty years of experience, with a combined experience of over fifty years.


We will give you an honest evaluation of your dental condition so you can make an informed decision on the best dental treatment for you. Our practice sees patients aged three to one hundred and includes families of four generations who continue to see us due to the trust we've earned and the consistent excellent dental care they have received over the years. Our goal is to become that family friend that you know and trust.


We would welcome the opportunity to provide you with exceptional dental care and include you as part of our extended dental family.
